Our Facilities and Capabilities


Anechoic Chamber

Our laboratory includes a 10m semi-anechoic chamber. This enables us to accurately measure Radiated Emissions (RE) as well as determine a product’s immunity to RF fields.

High Power Lab

From an isolated control room, we test withstand voltages up to 300kV, ac short circuit currents exceeding 40,000 Amps and impulse voltages of up to 600kV.

Superior Equipment

Vista Laboratories uses high-end testing equipment and performs routine calibrations in order to produce the most accurate results.
