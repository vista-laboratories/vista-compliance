Accreditation

We specialize in EMC/RF compliance testing and product certification for electrical equipment, electronic devices, and wireless communication products.

Vista Laboratories is ISO/IEC 17025 accredited as an electrical testing laboratory and is ISO/IEC 17065 accredited as a product certification body. We are also designated by NIST as a conformity assessment body for countries all over the world.

As a leader in this field, we provide comprehensive testing and certifications for radio frequency equipment, telecommunications terminal equipment (TTE), network equipment, information technology equipment (ITE), medical electrical equipment, household appliances, consumer electronic products, and audio / video products.
