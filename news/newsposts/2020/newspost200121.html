New Criteria on License-Exempt Radiocommunication Equipment

January 21, 2020

The Office of The National Broadcasting and Telecommunications Commission (NBTC) of Thailand announces new criteria for using frequency and radiocommunication equipment.

This notification summarizes multiple NBTC decisions/notifications issued previously on frequency allocation into one notification.

This will list license exempted frequencies which NBTC allow to use in general such as Wifi, RFID, NFC, etc.
