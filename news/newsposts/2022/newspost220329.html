Regulatory Framework for Short-Range Devices and Internet of Things

March 29th, 2022

The Pakistan Telecommunication Authority (PTA) issues new regulatory framework for short range devices and terrestrial internet of things services.

This framework lists all the allowed spectrum bands, as well as output power requirements.

Products operating in these frequency bands require type approval certification:
