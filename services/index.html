Markets and Services

Africa

Africa is comprised of more than 50 countries and is the second largest continent in terms of size and population, making it an appealing market.

Because of the assorted composition of countries, the approach can vary from country to country.

China

China, the world’s second largest economy has no official Mutual Recognition Agreement with any other countries.

Despite it being member of WTO, the regulatory framework in China can be intimidating if one attempts to accede to this market.

Russia

Russia, the world’s largest nation, borders European and Asian countries as well as the Pacific and Arctic oceans.

Getting into Russia market could be very challenge not only due to the language barrier but also the complicated certification schemes.

Saudi Arabia

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is the fastest growing telecom market in the Middle East.

The Communication and Information Technology Commission requires all radio frequency devices to be approved before being imported.

Taiwan

Just 50 years after the establishment of Taiwan's provincial government, the Bureau of Standards, Metrology, and Inspection was established for safety and compatibility.

Shortly after, the National Communications Commission was established for wireless and telecommunication devices.
