Brazilian Agency of Telecommunications, ANATEL, Enforcing New Technical Regulations

Posted on February 10, 2018 at 4:05 PM

Brazil has revoked 36 Resolutions that have been used for years and replaced them with ANATEL Acts. This action is under the published Resolution 686 and will come into force on Feb 12th, 2018. The ANATEL Acts to replace these resolutions have been issued on Feb 9th, 2018.

The summary list of the resolutions to be revoked and the respective ANATEL Acts as replacement can be found in following table:

Revoked Resolutions Description New Acts
Res. 533 Specific Absorption Rate (SAR) 955/2018
Res. 529 Electrical Safety 950/2018
Res. 481 Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC) 952/2018
Res. 481 Batteries/Chargers for Mobile Phones 951/2018
Other revoked resolutions include:
Res.299, Res.300, Res.306, Res.348, Res.359, Res.361, Res.370, Res.384, Res.390, Res.399, Res.414, Res.430, Res.467, Res.468, Res.470, Res.472, Res.476, Res.481, Res.482, Res.492, Res.498, Res.512, Res.542, Res.543, Res.554, Res.570, Res.572, Res.597, Res.601, Res.602, Res.603, Res.609, Res.610
